New Tarnished's Guide

Essential wisdom for those who have just arrived in the Lands Between. These tips will save you countless deaths and hours of frustration — including guidance for the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC.

01
⚔

Choose Your Starting Class Wisely

Foundation of your Tarnished

Your starting class determines your base stats but NOT your final build — any class can wield any weapon. Vagabond is the safest choice for beginners (high vigor, decent armor). Hero suits pure strength. Prophet for faith/healing. Astrologer for spellcasters who want offense from a distance.

💡 Pro Tip: The Wretch class starts at level 1 with balanced stats — best for veteran players who know exactly what build they want.

02
🗺

Explore Before Progressing

The open world is your greatest teacher

Elden Ring rewards curiosity. If a boss feels impossible, you're likely underleveled or under-geared. Explore Limgrave thoroughly — there are hidden caves, underground catacombs, and entire dungeons most players miss. Weeping Peninsula (south) has excellent early gear including the Bloodhound's Fang curved greatsword.

💡 Collect map fragments from stone pillars in each area — they'll reveal the layout of each region.

03
📊

Understanding Vigor & Leveling Priority

The most important stat for survival

Vigor (HP) is king in early-game. Aim for at least 40 Vigor before endgame, and 60 for NG+ or DLC. Endurance controls stamina and equip load — keep your load below 70% (medium roll) for optimal dodge iframes. Level your main damage stat to soft caps: 40 for most stats, 60 for Arcane and some Faith builds.

⚠ Warning: Neglecting Vigor is the #1 beginner mistake. Even veteran players struggle in the DLC without 55+ Vigor.

04
👥

Spirit Ashes & Summons

Your phantom army awaits

Spirit Ashes are one of Elden Ring's most powerful mechanics. The Mimic Tear (obtained in Nokron) creates a copy of your character — arguably the strongest summon in the game. Black Knife Tiche excels against slow bosses. Lhutel the Headless provides excellent distraction. Upgrade your ashes using Grave Glovewort and Ghost Glovewort at Roderika's shop in Roundtable Hold.

💡 For DLC bosses, summon signs near fog gates allow you to call NPC phantoms for free.

05
⚗

Crafting, Flasks & Consumables

Alchemy of survival

Allocate Flask of Wondrous Physick tears wisely — the Opaline Bubbletear (shield burst on low HP) and Stonebarb Cracked Tear (posture damage boost) are excellent all-purpose choices. Craft Neutralizing Boluses for poison, Stanching Boluses for bleed, and Stimulating Boluses for energy. Use Golden Seeds and Sacred Tears to upgrade your flask charges and potency.

Opaline Bubbletear Stonebarb Cracked Tear Crimsonburst Crystal Tear
06
🏰

Roundtable Hold & Multiplayer

The hub of the Tarnished

Roundtable Hold unlocks after reaching the First Step site of grace. Here you'll find Smithing Master Hewg (weapon upgrades), Roderika (spirit ash upgrades), Enia (trades Great Runes and Remembrances), and various merchants. For multiplayer, use Furlcalling Finger Remedy to see summon signs, or the Tarnished's Furled Finger to leave your own sign.

💡 Use passwords in multiplayer settings to summon friends directly without matching at random.

07
🌿

Shadow of the Erdtree — DLC Entry

The Realm of Shadow awaits

To access the DLC, you must first defeat Starscourge Radahn in Caelid AND interact with Mohg, Lord of Blood's cocoon in Mohgwyn Palace. The DLC introduces Scadutree Fragments (shadow power upgrades) and Revered Spirit Ashes — both are essential to collect. The DLC is significantly harder than the base game; recommended entry level: 150+ with 60 Vigor.

⚠ Scadutree Blessings are mandatory for DLC — without them, even regular enemies will two-shot you regardless of your level.

08
🎯

Status Effects & Weaknesses

Exploit enemy vulnerabilities

Most bosses are susceptible to at least one status effect. Bleed (Hemorrhage) deals massive percentage-based damage — highly effective on humanoid bosses. Frost reduces absorption and staggers. Scarlet Rot devastates large bosses. Gravity magic works on flying enemies. Check the Elden Ring wiki for specific weaknesses — the right tool makes any boss far more manageable.

Hemorrhage Frostbite Scarlet Rot Madness Sleep

Colossal Greatsword Overlord

Recommended Class: Hero / Vagabond

The definitive bruiser build. Wields enormous weapons that stagger most enemies with a single strike. Slow but devastating — each swing feels earned. Exceptionally strong against bosses susceptible to posture break. The introduction of Stormcaller and Savage Lion's Claw ash of war makes this build terrifying in the DLC.

Recommended Armor

Bull-Goat Set (max poise), Veteran's Armor, or mix-match for ~51 poise threshold. Use Radahn's Lion armor for mid poise with style.

Bleed Dex — Flowing Blade

Recommended Class: Samurai / Bandit

The fastest, most stylish build in Elden Ring. Dexterity weapons have the best animations and benefit massively from bleed infusion. The Rivers of Blood katana remains one of the strongest weapons in the game even after patches. Exceptionally mobile — use jumping attacks to deal massive poise damage.

Key Strategy

Dual wield two bleed katanas for maximum hemorrhage buildup. Bloodflame Blade Incantation adds bleed to any weapon. White Mask helm triggers when enemies bleed nearby. Use L1 (dual wield) spam for rapid status buildup.

The glass cannon. Intelligence builds deal astronomical damage when played correctly, but require significant knowledge of spell timings. Comet Azur with Cerulean Hidden Tear is legendary for decimating bosses in seconds. Lusat's Glintstone Staff provides the highest sorcery scaling in the game.

The dual seal casting method allows combining Lightning and Fire incantations for maximum versatility. Crucible Knight incantations add physical attacks that synergize beautifully with melee combat.

Blood Lord — Death Rite

Recommended Class: Bandit / Confessor

Arcane governs Item Discovery and scales Bleed, Scarlet Rot, Poison, and Death Blight. The Occult affinity scales purely with Arcane and doesn't reduce other scaling. Mohgwyn's Sacred Spear combined with Bloodboon incantations creates a devastating blood build. Death Rite sorceries are DLC exclusive and devastate undead-type enemies.
